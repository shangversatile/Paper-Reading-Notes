# RQ003: Prediction Quality versus Decision Quality

## Question ID

RQ003

## Research Question

Does the model with the lowest predictive error also minimize downstream allocation cost?

## Why It Matters

Operational decisions may prefer calibrated uncertainty or conservative behavior over the lowest point-forecast error.

## Hypothesis

Point-prediction ranking and decision-cost ranking may diverge under shift.

## Minimal Experiment

Map forecast outputs into simplified allocation policies and compare predictive metrics against downstream cost metrics.

## Required Assets

Forecast outputs, uncertainty estimates, allocation-cost definition, constraints, shift labels, and decision logs.

## Baselines

Mean Policy, Risk-Averse Policy, Upper-Quantile Policy, and simplified abstention or human-review policy.

## Metrics

Average decision cost, under-allocation cost, over-allocation cost, tail risk, CVaR, and constraint violation rate.

## Risks

Decision-cost definitions may be oversimplified, and policy conclusions may not generalize beyond the environmental forecasting use case.
