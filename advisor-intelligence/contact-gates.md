# Contact Gates

Research fit does not equal accessibility.

Do not infer recruiting status without official evidence.

Distinguish contact routes before outreach:

| Route Type | Meaning | Evidence Required | Status |
| ---------- | ------- | ----------------- | ------ |
| Current-student forms | Forms restricted to currently enrolled students. | Official page and date checked. | Candidate |
| Admitted-student forms | Forms restricted to admitted students. | Official page and date checked. | Candidate |
| Open-source contribution routes | Contribution-first path through public projects. | Official repo or maintainer instructions. | Candidate |
| Questionnaires | Pre-contact or lab-interest forms. | Official page and date checked. | Candidate |
| Course-first routes | Course enrollment or project route before research contact. | Official course or lab instruction. | Candidate |
| Ordinary email outreach | Standard concise research inquiry. | Official email source and date checked. | Candidate |

Record verification date for every claim.

Separate facts from inference in all notes.
