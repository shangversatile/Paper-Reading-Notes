# Conformal Prediction

## Scope

Study marginal coverage, exchangeability, split conformal, online conformal, adaptive conformal, and multi-step conformal methods.

## Core Concepts

* marginal coverage
* exchangeability
* split conformal
* online conformal
* adaptive conformal
* multi-step conformal

## Questions to Track

* What guarantee is provided, and under what assumption?
* How does exchangeability fail in temporal or spatial data?
* How should conformal methods be adapted under drift?
* What does conformal coverage mean for multi-step forecasting?

## Connection to Active Project

This track supports Split Conformal Prediction, adaptive conformal extensions, and coverage analysis under dynamic distribution shift.

## Long-Term Relevance

Conformal prediction provides a reusable reliability lens for forecasting, monitoring, and uncertainty-aware decision systems.
