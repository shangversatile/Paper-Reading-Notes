# Spatiotemporal Modeling

## Scope

Study temporal forecasting, graph construction, graph convolution, recurrent and convolutional temporal modeling, multi-step forecasting, and environmental and traffic forecasting.

## Core Concepts

* temporal forecasting
* graph construction
* graph convolution
* recurrent and convolutional temporal modeling
* multi-step forecasting
* environmental and traffic forecasting

## Questions to Track

* What assumptions define a spatiotemporal forecasting task?
* How should graph structure be constructed and validated?
* When do recurrent, convolutional, or hybrid temporal models fail?
* How do multi-step forecasts change reliability claims?

## Connection to Active Project

This track supports baseline relearning and independent model selection for reliable spatiotemporal forecasting.

## Long-Term Relevance

Spatiotemporal modeling remains a core foundation for environmental forecasting, traffic forecasting, and future reliable AI applications over structured temporal data.
